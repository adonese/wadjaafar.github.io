Rice

ITEMS
  1. Rice: more than 3 persons, buy 1KG
  2. Water: duh!
  3. Salt
  4. Bowl
HOW TO
  1. let the water boils
  2. put the rice inside the boiled water
  3. don't fuc*ing move it
  4. give it like 5 minutes or something, or if in hury
  5. add a full spoon of salt to the boiled water with rice
  6. give it another 5 minutes
  7. DONE

Rice with meat (tiny meat)

ITEMS
  1. Rice Items
  2. Meat, it should be tiny meats
  3. Pepper, lots of it babe
  4. شمار، كسبرة، فلفل، شطة حمرا
  5. Onion, garlic
  6. Tomato
  7. oid food
HOW TO

    Go To Rice Recipe

  1. Cut the onions into small pieces (it will accelerate the readiness of the dish)
  2. Cut the tomato, but it doesn't need to be small pieces. Fire will burn the hell out of it
  3. Put the oil in the bowl and let it burns or boils
  4. add the onion to it
  5. keep moving the onion around the oil until the onion has a yellowish color. Try to avoid buring the onion
  6. add the tomato to the onion and keep moving until the tomato starts to lose its shape
  7. add the rest of the component to the bowl: garlic, شمار، كسبرة، فلفل، شطة حمرا, and the rest
  8. When the boil components start to get together into a unified shape, you might want to stop here
  9. This will be your soup
  10. from here we were able to make the rice put it into a new bowl
  11. Finally, put the soup in the rice's bowl (the one you gonna eat from it
